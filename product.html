Labviq closes the gap between a physical material and its digital record — without adding a separate step to anyone's day.

What it does

From physical to digital, automatically.

Capture

Materials are captured as they enter the lab, in the flow of normal work.

Structure

Each one becomes a clean, structured record — with no manual typing.

Verify

Records are completed and cross-checked against trusted reference sources.

Connect

Every record stays connected to the work and the inventory it belongs to.

How it works

Three steps. No data entry.

STEP 01

Capture

Capture a material as it arrives.

STEP 02

Confirm

Confirm what's in front of you and move on.

STEP 03

Done

A complete record is created and filed automatically.

Everything else happens in the background. Review or adjust any record whenever you like — there's nothing to stop and fill in by hand.

Beyond intake

More than a one-time record.

Labviq isn't only for new arrivals. The same capture brings materials already in the lab into a single, consistent record — so you finally know what's actually there.

And because each record updates as materials are used and replaced, it reflects the real state of the lab rather than a snapshot from months ago.
